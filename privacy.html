Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

This UNIQLO Inventory System operates as a client-side web application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or inventory records on remote servers. All data you input is managed entirely within your browser.

2. Local Storage & Cookies

We use your browser's Local Storage exclusively to remember your preferences (such as acknowledging our cookie banner) so that you are not repeatedly interrupted. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are utilized.

3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your personal machine. We recommend securing your computer with a password and keeping your operating system up to date.
